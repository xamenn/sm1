Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

sm1 is an anonymous text chat platform available at sm1.online. We do not require accounts or personal information to use the service.

What data we collect

We do not store chat messages. Messages are relayed in real time between users and are never written to any database or disk. We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers. Server infrastructure may generate standard access logs that include IP addresses and timestamps. These logs are retained for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention purposes only.

Cookies

We do not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

Third parties

The service is hosted on Railway (railway.app). Their infrastructure processes network requests as part of normal operation. We do not share data with any other third parties.

Age requirement

sm1 is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect or process data from minors. If you believe a minor is using the platform, please contact us at abuse@sm1.online.

Abuse & contact

To report abuse or for any privacy-related questions, contact us at abuse@sm1.online.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
